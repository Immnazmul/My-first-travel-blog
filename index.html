My Travel Blog

Let me share the remarkable story of my first international internship at the age of 18, which transformed me in ways I could never have imagined. As a typical shy, reserved, and under-confident South Indian girl, I had never truly opened up to myself or others. Eager to discover my true self and unlock my potential, embarking on this solo journey abroad turned out to be the best decision of my life.

Before boarding the flight, I decided to make this adventure a thrilling experience. I realized that traveling to a distant land where I knew no one presented the perfect opportunity to shed my ordinary persona and embrace a new personality. After all, there would be no familiar faces to pass judgment, and even if they did, it wouldn't matter since I wouldn't see them again! I devised a set of rules to follow: refraining from researching the destination, befriending everyone I met through the project, speaking confidently even if I made mistakes in English, embracing humor (a quality I had often lacked), exploring famous places, avoiding conflicts, nurturing humility and empathy, and maintaining an open and non-judgmental attitude towards others. With these guidelines in mind, I landed in the country, where a kind gentleman picked me up from the airport, marking the beginning of my incredible journey.
